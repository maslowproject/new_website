Why We’re Here?

Moving is stressful. Believe us, we know! With moving comes a lot of worries, fears, and headaches. A series of never-ending questions rush into your mind like, “Do I really need this where I’m going? Are my neighbors jerks? Is this the right move?” There could be any number of answers to those questions, but when it comes to the service you should use to move or transport your items we believe that there is only one clear-cut answer, Trucke! At Trucke we pride ourselves on making your moving experience as comfortable and affordable as possible. Your satisfaction is at the core of everything we do, because we’re not just transitioning your items, we’re helping you transition to the next phase of your life. Here at Trucke our mission is clear: we aim to make your next move your best move.

Where we’re located?

Trucke is currently available in College Station, TX, but we will be expanding to additional markets. To request Trucke to be added in your city send us an email at contactus@getatrucke.com and let us know you want to be added to an interest list for drivers and customers.
